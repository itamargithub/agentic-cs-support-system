# Fees & Refunds Policy (KB-003)
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01

## Common Fees
- Monthly account maintenance:
  - Checking: €4.99/month (waived if salary ≥ €1,000/month)
  - Savings: €0/month
- Card replacement:
  - Standard: €8
  - Express: €15
- International transfer fee: €12 + FX spread (varies)

## Overdrafts & Penalties
- Overdraft fee: €7 per occurrence
- Late loan payment fee: €10
- Interest continues to accrue on overdue balances.

## Refunds
### When Refunds Are Allowed
Refunds may be issued for:
- Duplicate fees caused by system error
- Fees charged during an outage impacting customer access
- Proven bank-side processing mistakes

### When Refunds Are Not Allowed (Typical)
- Customer-caused overdrafts
- Validly disclosed monthly fees (unless eligible waiver criteria were met)

### Refund Timeline
- Card/fee refunds: typically 3–5 business days after approval
- Transfer fee refunds: 5–10 business days (requires investigation)

## Rep Guidance
- Always document: date, fee type, customer impact, and reason for exception request.
- If outage-related: check outage bulletin and reference it in the ticket.
