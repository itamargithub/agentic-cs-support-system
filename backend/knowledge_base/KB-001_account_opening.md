# Account Opening Policies (KB-001)
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01

## Overview
This document summarizes the bank’s standard requirements and processing steps for opening personal accounts.

## Accepted Account Types
- Checking (Personal)
- Savings (Personal)
- Student accounts (ages 16–25, proof required)
- Joint accounts (two account holders)

## Eligibility & Identification
A customer must provide:
1. Government-issued photo ID (passport or national ID)
2. Proof of address (utility bill/bank statement issued within last 3 months)
3. Tax identification number (if applicable by jurisdiction)

### Non-Resident Customers
Non-residents may open **Savings only** unless they provide:
- Local address proof, and
- Additional due diligence (KYC Enhanced Review)

## Minimum Deposit & Activation
- Checking: minimum initial deposit €50
- Savings: minimum initial deposit €20
- Account is activated when the deposit posts and KYC is approved.

## KYC & Screening
- Name screening against sanctions/PEP lists is mandatory.
- If screening flags: account is **pending review** (up to 5 business days).
- If KYC cannot be completed: decline and provide standard notice.

## Processing Time Targets
- In-branch: typically same day (if documents complete)
- Online: 1–2 business days
- Enhanced review: up to 5 business days

## Common Rep Guidance
- If customer lacks address proof: recommend digital statements or alternative acceptable proofs.
- If customer is in a hurry: offer in-branch appointment and ensure documents are ready.
