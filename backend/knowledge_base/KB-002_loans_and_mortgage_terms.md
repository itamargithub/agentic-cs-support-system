# Loans & Mortgage Terms (KB-002)
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-15

## Personal Loans
### Key Terms
- Typical APR: 6.5%–14.9% (risk-based)
- Loan amounts: €1,000–€50,000
- Tenors: 12–84 months
- Early repayment: allowed with up to 1% fee (see KB-003 Fees)

### Approval Criteria (Summary)
- Credit score threshold (internal model)
- Debt-to-income ratio (DTI) within policy
- Stable income verification (2–3 payslips or equivalent)

## Mortgages
### Key Terms
- Fixed rate options: 2, 5, 10 years
- Variable rate: base rate + margin
- Typical LTV max: 80% (primary residence)
- Processing: 2–6 weeks depending on property valuation and documentation

### Fees (High-level)
- Valuation fee may apply
- Legal/registration fees vary by jurisdiction

## Payment Issues
- If a payment is late: late fee may apply and interest accrues (see KB-003).
- Hardship: offer payment plan review and document customer circumstances.

## Rep Guidance
- Do not quote a guaranteed APR; provide a range and explain it depends on underwriting.
- For mortgages, emphasize timelines and required documents early to reduce delays.
