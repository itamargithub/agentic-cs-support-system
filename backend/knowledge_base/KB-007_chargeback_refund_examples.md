# Chargeback & Refund Examples (KB-007)
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01

## Example 1: Unauthorized online transaction
- Customer reports unfamiliar €120 charge.
- Rep freezes card, opens dispute case.
- Provisional credit may be issued in 1–3 business days.
- Investigation may take 10–45 days.

## Example 2: Duplicate monthly fee due to system error
- Customer charged twice for checking maintenance.
- Confirm billing records show duplicate.
- Approve refund; timeline 3–5 business days.

## Example 3: Customer overdraft fee dispute
- Customer went negative due to overspending.
- Refund not typically allowed unless bank-side processing mistake is proven.

## Rep Guidance
- Use examples to set expectations and reduce escalations.
